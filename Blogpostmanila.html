INDUSTRY IMMERSION TOUR

A Journey Through Manila's Tech Scene & Culture

Join me as I share highlights from our 8-day immersion tour in Manila! We explored 6 innovative tech companies, discovered iconic tourist spots, and learned about the rich history and vibrant culture of the city. Here’s a day-by-day look at our adventure:

Day 1

We landed at NAIA Terminal 3, excited for the week ahead! After meeting our tour guide, we explored the Mall of Asia, then checked into our dorm to rest. That evening, we enjoyed a welcome dinner and shared our hopes for the tour with classmates.

Day 2

Our first company stop: FOUNDEVER! We met their team, learned about their projects, and got a feel for their work culture. In the afternoon, we visited the Google office—an inspiring experience seeing how a global tech giant operates up close.

Day 3

We visited Teleperformance and discovered how they manage customer service at scale. Later, at Kollab, we learned about company culture, portfolio tips, and how AI is shaping the tech industry. It was a day full of insights!

Day 4

Our final day of company visits took us to ADB (Asian Development Bank), where we learned about their mission and toured their impressive offices. We wrapped up with Microsourcing, discovering their projects and how they support clients worldwide.

Day 5

We traveled to Tagaytay and visited People’s Park in the Sky, soaking in breathtaking views. Skyranch brought out our adventurous side with fun rides, and we ended the day shopping and dining at Trinoma Mall.

Day 6

Bags packed, we set off for Baguio City! The scenic bus ride was a highlight. After checking in, we relaxed at Burnham Park and later explored the night market, sampling delicious local dishes.

Day 7

Free day! We explored Baguio City, hiked to SM City Baguio (famous for its natural ventilation), and enjoyed the cool mountain air. That evening, we returned to Quezon City to prepare for our journey home.

Day 8

We said our goodbyes and checked out, but my friends and I stayed a few extra days to explore Manila further. We visited Intramuros, Rizal Park, and other landmarks, tasted more Filipino food, and picked up souvenirs to remember this amazing trip.
